What is Clirooo?

- Web application can know the information of Tokyo Metro.

Based on open data that has been disclosed in the Tokyo Metro app contest, I've created an app that seen in the terminal screen information of Tokyo Metro.
You can call a variety of data and information transfer delay assistance, such as number of passengers.

- Is it an app to whom it was directed?

It is a web app for engineers who has lived in Tokyo, to use the Tokyo Metro.

- And How do I use the Clirooo?

As always, engineers will be able to hit the terminal on the web, to obtain a variety of information.
For details on how to use and command, refer to the document page or "help command".
